About Us

Cyber Secured India

Cyber Secured India is an initiative by Feathers CSI Foundation which is registered as a non profit company.

Cyber Secured India has been working in training, internship and mentoring since 2014. We have trained more than 2,00,000 people on various domains of cyber security and digital forensics, which includes school and college students, law enforcement agencies, corporate companies and many more.
